.._TV: IRL the Series_
IRL: The Series, a webseries in which Roselyn plays the sharp & adventurous Tanya, is an official selection for the [[https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10150750148524981&set=gm.1842850985952137&type=3&theater Los Angeles Film Awards]].  
And IRL is a semi-finalist in the Los Angeles CineFest! Keep up to date with the  [[https://www.facebook.com/IRLtheSeries/photos/a.1653373114910091.1073741828.1638435516403851/1842595782654489/?type=3&theater/ IRL facebook page]].

.._Theatre_
Roselyn is prepping for a summer of theatre. She's playing Cleopatra! In a new comedy by Kyle McDonald, called Macbeth's Head, Cleopatra teams up with Richard III to take revenge on Shakespeare for their tragic ends. It's going to be hilarious! Come & see, July 5-15 in the [[http://fringetoronto.com/fringe-festival/ Toronto Fringe Festival]]
Roselyn's dream roles include Cleopatra in Shakespeare's Antony & Cleopatra, Helena in _All's Well That Ends Well_ and Hermione in _Harry Potter and the Cursed Child_... obviously.
.._Quiver Theatre_
-Roselyn is workshopping Electra, by Sophocles, the Anne Carson translation with [[https://www.quivertheatre.com/ Quiver Theatre]] -- building poetic works into physical theatre.
//-This new company creates movement pieces based on poetic works. Roselyn is working with them on developing QT's inaugural season. 

.._Writing_
With Livia Berius, Roselyn is co-writing a script about the juggle that is life for a woman trying to succeed in acting and in law - at the same time. It's a dramedy that digs into the misogyny and racial prejudices in professional settings in Toronto. Think [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G1qYxOF7TUs "Insecure"]] meets [[http://www.cbs.com/shows/the_good_wife/ "The Good Wife"]] meets [[http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1742650/ "I don't know how she does it"]].  

For more news, join the facebook group:  [[https://www.facebook.com/groups/sixandeight/ Roselyn, Actor & Storyteller]].
