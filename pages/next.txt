.._TV: IRL the Series_
Exciting news! IRL: The Series, a webseries in which Roselyn plays the sharp & adventurous Tanya, is up for an ACTRA Award! Best Toronto Series Ensemble, so each actor would be part of a win. 
IRL is a semi-finalist in the Los Angeles CineFest! Keep up to date with the  [[https://www.facebook.com/IRLtheSeries/photos/a.1653373114910091.1073741828.1638435516403851/1842595782654489/?type=3&theater/ IRL facebook page]].

.._Theatre_
Roselyn is prepping for a summer of theatre. Her dream roles include Cleopatra in Shakespeare's Antony & Cleopatra, Helena in _All's Well That Ends Well_ and Hermione in _Harry Potter and the Cursed Child_... obviously.
