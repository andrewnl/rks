.._Film/TV_
Roselyn stars in the pilot of [[http://failbetterseries.com Fail Better]], a webseries about fear of failure in a 30-year-old articling student. The series follows Tegan through her struggle to prove herself and become a lawyer, and to maintain the creative person she is through it all. Episode 01 is in post-production now! Check in [[https://www.facebook.com/FailBetter/ on the "Fail Better" facebook page]] for updates as the series develops.

.._Writing_
Roselyn is also the creator and co-writer, with Livia Berius, of [[http://failbetterseries.com Fail Better]], a webseries about fear of failure in a 30-year-old articling student. It's a dramedy that digs into the misogyny and racial prejudices in professional environments in Toronto. Have a read to learn about [[https://www.failbetterseries.com/writers/ Roselyn & Livia's journeys to telling this story]] and to the [[https://www.failbetterseries.com/inspirations/ inspirations behind it]]. Think [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G1qYxOF7TUs Insecure]] meets [[http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1442462/ The Good Wife]].

Roselyn also has some plays in the works. For news, join the facebook group:  [[https://www.facebook.com/groups/sixandeight/ Roselyn, Actor & Storyteller]]. 

.._Theatre_
Roselyn recently finished a comedic turn, playing Cleopatra. (Figure that one out!) In _Macbeth's Head_, a new comedy by Kyle McDonald, Cleopatra teams up with Maccers & Richard III to make Shakespeare fix their tragic ends. Stay tuned for the future of this Cleopatra [[http://www.malfiproductions.com/macbeths-head at Malfi Productions]].
